How to Choose the Right Laptop

RAM

More RAM allows smoother multitasking. 8GB is minimal for students, 16GB recommended for heavy multitaskers and content creators.

SSD vs HDD

SSD is much faster and more reliable. Prefer NVMe SSDs for snappy performance.

GPU (Graphics)

Discrete GPUs are important for gaming and creative apps like Premiere / Blender. Integrated GPUs suffice for office and school tasks.

Processor types

Modern Intel/AMD CPUs: prioritize higher single-core speeds for everyday use; multi-core for heavy rendering. Apple M-series balances both with efficiency.
