How to Choose the Right Laptop

RAM affects multitasking. 8GB for basic use, 16GB for most power users, 32GB+ for heavy workloads.

SSDs are faster and more reliable; prefer NVMe SSD for OS and main apps. HDDs are fine for large media storage.

CPU cores & IPC matter for productivity; discrete GPUs help with creative software and gaming. Balance according to workload.
